CAMPAIGN POSTERS



This is till date my most favourite projects out of all. Here, we were supposed to take any social campaign (education for tribal kids is mine) and marry that to any brand (camlin for me). These were then supposed to be converted into posters, but by only using Indian art forms. I chose Warli, madhubani, gond and santhal art because these are all tribal art forms and it fit with my theme. I am noe going to animate one of these posters, which is an ongoing project. This process was really fun and I learnt a lot! These are some process pictures.
