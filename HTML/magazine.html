Magazine Design



This project was my favourite out of all I did in my first year of college. We had to collect all the data by oursleves by google forms/ asking questions to family members/ neighbours etc. This was our micro-level research. For macro-level which was cities and countries, we took help of google and also interviewed some strangers. After collection of all this data, we had to translate all of this information into a 16-page A5 magazine (front and back page included).
