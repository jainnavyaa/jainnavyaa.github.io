Tri-Fold Brochure



For this project, we had to make a tri-fold brochure for an imaginary city which we made up by ourselves. So, from the point of thinking of all the elements of the city, making it up fully to designing each and everything in the brochure, was done by me. It was really fun because the process was so interesting and also this colour palette and style was a little out of my comfort zone, so it was fun to experiment. Here are some process pictures!
