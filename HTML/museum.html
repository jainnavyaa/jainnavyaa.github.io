MUSEUM ROOM DESIGN (on sketchup)



This was my first sketcup project which had an amazing outcome. I started off by selecting certain ovjects from different musemums like the MET etc. I, then wrote a narrative and a concept note connecting all of these objects together. Proceeding further, I made a basi structure on paper and on sketchup, then the final one with the objects, lighting and everything on sketchup. I loved and enjoyed the process so much which is why the outcome was so good. I am really proud of this piece as I never thought I could ever do something like this. Here's some process pictures.
