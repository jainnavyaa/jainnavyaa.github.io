Passions



DANCE



I have always been really very passionate about
dance and I'm actually good at it. Since a very
young age, I have liked dressing up and dacning my
heart out. My mom tells me, when I was around 3/4
years old, I would put on my favourite ghaghra
and dance my heart out on my favourite song at
that time 'Kajra re'.



That's when my dance journey began. Seeing my love for dancing my mom made my join Kathak classes. I fell in love with this dance form almost immediately. I have been practicing Kathak since the age of 8 and still am a huge fan of it. These classes became my favourite part of the week. I did several competitions and stageshows with my guru who I am forever thankful to. She made me the dancer I am today. I will always owe that to her. She pushed me when I was down because she saw the potential in me. Because of this classical dance, the basics of dancing became really strong for me which is why now, I can do any form of dance very easily.

Now whenever I listen to music, I easily make up dances up in my head and if someone tells me to dance to a song I know on the spot, I'll be able to do it easily. In dj parties, I am the only who knows all the hooksteps to every song played. I can also learn choregraphies by just looking at them for 10-15 times. My favourite way to past time is by learning new dances from youtube everyday, or just by closing the curtains, locking myslef in a room, putting out loud music and dancing my heart out. Thats theraputic for me. I will never stop dancing!
